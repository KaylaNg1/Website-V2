About Me

I have always been a lover of disney movies and fantasy books, which explains my booming imagination and drive to create. My passion for computer science came later after entering my freshman year of high school.

My favorite thing about programming is the challenge. The feeling of victory after finally finding the bug in the code. In order to expand my knowledge on the wide range of Computer Science fields, I take all of the CS courses I can get my hands on and participate in as many CS extracurriculars as possible. My favorite experience has been being apart of my school's robotics team where I helped program the robot for the 2019-2020 FRC competition season.

When I'm not programming away, I like to read. A perfect day for me would be reading a book on a chilly Autumn day, wrapped snuggly in blankets.
